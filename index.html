Frequently Asked Questions

How is this different from other lead gen services?
Most lead gen services send generic templates to bought lists. We use AI to research each prospect individually and write emails that reference specific things about their business — recent news, awards, location changes. This gets 3-5x higher reply rates than generic outreach.
How quickly will I see results?
Most clients see their first positive replies within 2 weeks of campaign launch. Booked calls typically start appearing by week 3-4. We set realistic expectations: 10-25 qualified prospects per month, not hundreds of bad leads.
What industries do you work with?
We work with local and regional businesses: dental/medical practices, law firms, financial advisors, home services, fitness/wellness, real estate, and B2B service companies. If you sell to other businesses or high-value consumers, we can help.
Do I need to provide my own email list?
No. We find the prospects, research them, and build the list for you. You don't need any existing data. We start from scratch and identify your ideal clients using AI.
Are there contracts or long-term commitments?
No contracts. Month-to-month. Cancel anytime with 7 days notice. We earn your business every month with results, not lock-in.
How much does it cost to get started?
Plans start at $750/month with no setup fees. We require payment before the first month's campaign begins. We do not offer performance-based pricing at this time.
